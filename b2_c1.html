Oi, Brasil! - Língua e Cultura B2 Livro-Texto (B2/C1) - Student's Edition is the intermediate-advanced level text book of the Brazilian Portuguese language learning series Oi, Brasil!. It contains modern dialogues, comprehension exercises, grammar, Brazilian culture and two downloadable CDs. It covers levels B2 and C1 according to the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR).

The Oi, Brasil! - Língua e Cultura – B2 – Livro-Texto (B2/C1) volume is divided into 12 thematic units. It contains modern everyday dialogues, dialogue comprehension exercises, schematic grammar, elements of Brazilian culture, the solutions of the dialogue comprehension exercises, and two downloadable CDs in mp3 format.
