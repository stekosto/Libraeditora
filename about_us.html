About us

Libra Editora is a small publishing house specialized in Brazilian Portuguese as a foreign language and in Brazilian culture. Currently, it provides globally Oi, Brasil!, a Brazilian Portuguese teaching/learning series for levels A1-C1, a method that, since its first edition, increasingly gains ground in the field of Brazilian Portuguese learning. The Oi, Brasil! series is addressed to teachers and students of all nationalities, as it is entirely written in Brazilian Portuguese. The Libra Editora Customer Service Team will be happy to receive any comments and answer to any questions you may have regarding the Oi, Brasil! series or our future publications.
