Kelly Samiotou

Kelly (Kalliopi) Samiotou has studied Literature, Fine Art, and Film Directing. She holds a BA degree in Italian Language and Literature, and three MA degrees in: Spanish and Portuguese; Spanish, Portuguese, and Latin American Studies; and Form and History of Visual Arts. Currently, she is a Ph.D. candidate in Spanish and Portuguese, and Comparative Media Analysis and Practice at Vanderbilt University, where she is an Instructor of Record of Spanish and Portuguese, and a Teaching Assistant of Film and Media Arts. She has been a Roman's language Instructor at the Athens University of Economics and Business, and has taught languages and culture at various schools and multinational companies. She has also worked on Digital Humanities, translation, directing, and dubbing for several years, and has participated in projects and conferences in Latin America, Europe and the United States. She is the author of the Brazilian Portuguese teaching seriesOi, Brasil!.
