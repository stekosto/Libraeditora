COMPONENTS OF THE COURSE

Oi, Brasil! - Língua e Cultura - A1/A2/B1 PACK contains modern dialogues, grammar, exercises, oral practice, Brazilian culture and two CDs. It covers levels A1, A2 and B1 according to the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR).

Oi, Brasil! - Língua e Cultura - A1/A2/B1 is divided into 11 thematic and 3 revision units. It contains modern everyday dialogues, dialogue comprehension exercises, schematic grammar, grammar and vocabulary exercises, listening comprehension exercises, pronunciation exercises, oral practice exercises, elements of Brazilian culture, and two CDs (also downloadable in mp3 format).

The Oi, Brasil! – Soluções e Transcrições - A1/A2/B1 contains the solutions of all the exercises of the Oi, Brasil! – Língua e Cultura – A1/A2/B1 volume, and the transcriptions of the listening comprehension exercises.
